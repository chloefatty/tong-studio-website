What is Gong Bath?

Gong bath is a meditative sound experience that uses vibrational tones from bronze gongs to release stress and rebalance the nervous system. Our studio offers a tranquil, acoustically treated space with integrated aromatherapy, soft lighting, and certified sound therapists guiding every session.

Session Schedule

We run three micro-immersion sessions Monday to Friday at 12:45, 17:45, and 19:00, and restorative 60-minute sessions on weekends. Classes are limited to 8 participants for optimal sonic resonance and personal attention.
